Client Project - Site Promotion Proposal

Target Audience

  • Prospective employers including:
    • HR resources from companies in the nutrition space
    • Recruiters
  • Fellow colleagues (Networking possibilities)
Objective Strategy Cost
Increase site's visibility in professionally oriented social media. CPC adds on LinkedIn targeting nutrition-related industries and/or profiles with job titles including Recruiter, Nutritionist, etc. Achieving as much 50 clicks per month from a pool of professionals will translate into a high change for a job opportunity. $2 per click with a limit of $100 per month.
Increase site's visibility in main-stream social media. Budgeted ads on Facebook targeting users with nutrition-related profiles and/or who display and interest in nutrition-related topics. The change for a job opportunity is less compared to LinkedIn but will give the opportunity for networking and entrepreneurial opportunities. The goal will be to reach close to 30 clicks per month. $1 per day budget
Increase site's visibility in search engines. Post quality nutrition-related information to highly visited sites like Reddit and/or other blogging engines. which will link back to the client's site. The effectiveness of this strategy will depend on the client being pro-active enough to include the links and the quality of her information. No monetary cost. Time and effort on the client's part to publish quality posts
