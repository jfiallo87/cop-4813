Client Project - Use Case(s)

Raisa Cavalcante (henceforth known as the "client") has requested a basic "professional profile" web site (henceforth known as the "web site") to increase her visibility to "prospective employers". The web site will consist of the following:

  • A home page containing her Resume (Summary, Education, Professional Experience, etc.)
  • A contact form allowing prospective employers to get in touch with the client

Use Case: Contact by Prospective Employer

Main Success Scenario

  1. Prospective employer lands on the web site's home page
  2. Prospective employer decides to contact the client
  3. Prospective employer follows link to contact information
  4. Prospective employer contacts the client

Extensions

  1. Prospective employer decides not to contact the client
    1. Prospective employer navigates away from the web site
